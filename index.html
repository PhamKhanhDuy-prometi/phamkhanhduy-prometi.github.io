Coding Inspiration 2026: Smart Life

CODING INSPIRATION 2026 (5th season) is an academic competition organized by JS Club for IT students to enhance their problem-solving skills, teamwork, and personal portfolios.

Smart Life – Applying technology (Web, App, IoT, embedded systems, etc.) to solve daily issues and optimize human convenience, health, and safety.

Format

Consists of 4 stages:

  1. Registration
  2. Ideation
  3. Implimentation
  4. Presentation
The finals feature a 3-hour continuous coding sub-challenge and a bonus point system for complex features.

Key benefits

  1. Learning & Development: Access to an exclusive Tech Talk on Hackathon strategies and hands-on experience in building real products (Web, App, software).
  2. Attractive Prizes: Total prize pool worth up to 12 million VND along with various gifts from the Organizers and Sponsors.
  3. Networking & Challenge: Connect with a tech-passionate community, sharpen presentation skills before the Judges, and tackle a unique 3-hour continuous coding challenge in the Finals.
  4. Career Boost: Upgrade your CV and Portfolio with a practical project to stand out to employers and prove your capabilities.
